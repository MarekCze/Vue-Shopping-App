Our Goals

Timepiece.com exists solely to help people discover art in arguably its' most intriguing form, timepieces. Watches don't only serve the purpose of telling time, they are also a form of self expression. We are here to open the doors for you into this magical world.

Our History

We have not been around long. The website was created last Thursday, but in 50 years time we will be able to say that we've been a source of inspiration and masterfully crafted watches for generations.

Our Future

As long as we will have watches to show to the curious minds around the world, this websites future is set in stone. We will go bankrupt in three years and no one will have heard of us because this is just a project for college and will never see the light of day.
